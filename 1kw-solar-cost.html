1 kW Solar System Cost in India

1 kW rooftop solar system చిన్న ఇళ్ల electricity consumption కోసం suitable system. రోజుకు సుమారు 4–5 units electricity generate చేయగలదు.

Cost Details

  • Total system cost → ₹60,000 – ₹80,000
  • Government subsidy → ₹30,000
  • Final cost → ₹30,000 – ₹50,000

Electricity Generation

  • Daily generation → 4 to 5 units
  • Monthly generation → 120 to 150 units
  • Yearly generation → 1500 units approx

Suitable For

Small homes, shops, offices కోసం 1 kW solar system మంచి option. Electricity bill తగ్గించడానికి rooftop solar మంచి investment.
